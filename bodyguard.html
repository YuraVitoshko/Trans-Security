Why You Might Need Private Security Protection

Many people might assume that armed bodyguards simply specialize in reacting to potential threats in a rapid and efficient way. The reality is that a trained personal security guard has skills that go well beyond that rapid response to danger. At ISS, our private security guards are trained to:

  • Quickly identify warning signs
  • Develop plans to secure your person if danger manifests, including mapping escape routes when necessary

  • Advise you on risk mitigation strategies to reduce the potential for danger
